Nutrition

Exercise & Mental Stimulation

Regular physical activity keeps joints flexible and weight controlled. Pair walks or play sessions with brain games like simple training drills or puzzle feeders to prevent boredom. Consistent stimulation helps reduce unwanted behaviors.

Health & Grooming

Routine vet checkups catch problems early and build a health baseline. Brushing, nail trimming, and dental care support comfort and hygiene while strengthening your bond. Watch for changes in appetite, energy, or coat condition as early signals.
