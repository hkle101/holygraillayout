About the Show

Friends is an iconic American sitcom that follows the lives of six close friends — Ross, Rachel, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe — as they navigate adulthood, relationships, careers, and everyday life in New York City.

The show is beloved for its humor, memorable catchphrases, and strong character chemistry. Whether it’s Ross yelling “We were on a break!” or Joey asking, “How you doin’?,” Friends delivers both comedy and heartfelt moments.

Fun Facts

  • Friends premiered in 1994 and ran for 10 seasons.
  • Central Perk became one of TV’s most iconic coffee shops.
  • Phoebe’s song “Smelly Cat” is still a fan favorite.
  • The cast negotiated equal pay starting in season 3.
