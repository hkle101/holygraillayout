StarBucks Knockoff Recipe

Instructions

  • Each Card shows a Starbucks Knockoff Drink
  • You can flip the card to see the recipe
  • Enjoy making your favorite Starbucks drinks at home!

Caramel

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 tbsp caramel sauce
  • 1-2 tsp sugar (optional)

Strawberrycofee

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/4 cup strawberry syrup
  • Ice cubes

Matcha

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp matcha green tea powder
  • 2 oz hot water
  • 6 oz milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1-2 tsp sweetener (honey, sugar, or syrup)
